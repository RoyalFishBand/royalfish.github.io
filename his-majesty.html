History

The title was revealed via a public game of hangman, hosted on the Royal Fish Neocities website. The Instagram post with the final winning guess, set to "Celebration" by Kool and the Gang, stated that the album will release on August 27th, 2025. This is the first day of classes at the College of William and Mary, aligning with the normal Royal Fish release pattern.

On the morning of May 30th, 2025, the Instagram account posted a story that claimed "royal fish is making danceable music in 2025." This referred to the upcoming album release.

Early on August 14th, 2025, the Instagram account revealed the official album cover.
