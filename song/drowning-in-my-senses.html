"Drowning in My Senses" is the third and longest track on Royal Fish. It is a considerable shift in tone from "Forest of Noise" and "How Long," and the lyrics seem to be about depression or overstimulation, because of its paradoxical description of the speaker's sensory abilities and the song's title.
