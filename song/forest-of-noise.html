Release: Royal Fish
Genres: Indie Rock, Pop Rock, Psychedelic Pop
Run Time: 3:36
Credits:
Emily Moore: Production, Guitar
Ellie Minor: Drums

"Forest of Noise" is the first track on Royal Fish. It heavily features electric guitar and organ.


On October 3rd, 2025, the band played this song live for the first time. Emily mentioned her personal dislike for the song, because of its persisting popularity, even over newer releases.
