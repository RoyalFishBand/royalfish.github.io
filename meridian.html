The Meridian Coffeehouse

The Meridian Coffeehouse is a venue located at 206 S. Boundary Street that is part of the campus of the College of William & Mary. It hosts a variety of both campus-based and touring bands, as well as events like craft and movie nights. For more information, visit their Instagram.

Relevant History
