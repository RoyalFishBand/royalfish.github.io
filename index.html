          Royal Fish is a band from the College of William & Mary. A self-proclaimed "variety band," they play covers of music from the 1960s through today of varying genres. Their original music, which they also perform live, has been likened by listeners to Pink Floyd, The Beatles, The Cocteau Twins, and The Doors. The band's current lineup consists of Emily, Major Ellie, and Dan Landry. Royal Fish has released two studio albums and one EP.
