Rules of the Wiki

  1. Only add correct, relevant information

    2. Provide a source wherever you can. If your edit is deemed to be incredible, we may ask for a source or remove it. Learn how to reference sources

  2. Stay consistent with the wiki's style

    3. Don't change fonts, colors, or standard image/text orientations

  3. Don't grant unauthorized edit access.

    4. Anyone can edit, but they need to go through us.

  4. No explicit material

    5. This includes sexually explicit content, nudity, and profanity (except in the case of a direct quote.) References to substances are always allowed.

  5. No editorializing

    6. This is an archive of the band's history. We do not currently have a place for general discussion of the band, but we may create one.
